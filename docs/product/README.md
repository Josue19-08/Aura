## Quick Summary

**Problem:** Counterfeit products cost industries billions and endanger consumers. Traditional tracking systems are centralized and can be tampered with.

**Solution:** Aura creates an immutable product identity on blockchain. Every transfer is recorded permanently. Anyone can verify authenticity.

**Key Benefits:**
- ✅ **Immutable** — History cannot be altered
- 🔍 **Transparent** — Complete custody chain visible
- 🚨 **Fraud Detection** — Automatic anomaly alerts
- 🌐 **Public Verification** — No special access needed
