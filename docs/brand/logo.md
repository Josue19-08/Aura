# Logo

## Concept

The Aura logo is the letter **A** with an integrated tracking pin at its apex. The pin features a solid center point surrounded by two concentric circles that evoke a signal pulse or radar.

**The icon works standalone** — no text required.

## Design Principles

### Form
- **Base:** Clean, geometric letter A
- **Pin:** Location marker integrated at the apex
- **Signal:** Concentric circles suggesting transmission/tracking
- **Connection:** Subtle line fusing pin to letter

### Style
- Thin outline, monochrome
- No solid fill except the pin's center point
- Works on dark and light backgrounds

## Color Variations

### Primary (Dark Background)
- **Pin center:** Signal `#00E5CC`
- **Outline:** White `#F0F0F5`
- **Background:** Void `#0A0A0F`

### Inverse (Light Background)
- **Pin center:** Signal `#00E5CC`
- **Outline:** Void `#0A0A0F`
- **Background:** White

## Sizing Guidelines

### Minimum Sizes
- **Digital:** 24px height minimum
- **Print:** 0.5 inch (12.7mm) height minimum

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space equal to the height of the letter A around all sides.

## Usage Rules

### DO:
- Use on Void `#0A0A0F` background (primary)
- Maintain aspect ratio when scaling
- Use high-contrast versions for accessibility

### DON'T:
- Don't rotate or distort
- Don't change the pin center color
- Don't add effects (shadows, gradients, glows)
- Don't place on busy backgrounds
